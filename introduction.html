Introduction & Mission

Investigative Idiot is an independent, source-first journal. The aim is simple: examine the public actions of celebrities, athletes, and other figures with clarity and proof.

Approach

  • Claims are backed by public records, credible reporting, official statements, and archived posts.
  • Privacy is respected — no speculation on private life unless it’s directly relevant to public actions.
  • Facts, context, and interpretation are clearly separated.
  • Corrections are welcomed and made promptly upon verification.

About the Author

Run by an anonymous researcher to keep focus on the work, not the person. No sponsorships or paid placements.

Legal & Ethics

All content is for informational purposes and relies on publicly available sources cited in each piece. No allegations are made beyond what sources support.
