Introduction & Mission

Investigative Idiot is an independent, source-first journal focused on public actions of celebrities, athletes, and other figures.

Approach

  • Claims backed by public records, credible reporting, and archived posts.
  • Privacy respected—no speculation on private life unless directly relevant.
  • Facts, context, and interpretation clearly separated.
  • Corrections welcomed and posted after verification.

Author

Run by an anonymous researcher—keeps the focus on the work, not the person.

Legal & Ethics

For informational purposes. Based on publicly available sources cited in each piece. No allegations beyond what sources support.
